steps toward perfection

Found bugs? Please help by reporting the bugs in FastLZ issue tracker. Click on New Issue (on the top left), and then enter the short summary and bug description.

Before filing a bug, please read the FAQ and search for existing bugs.

feature request

Go to issue tracker. Click on New Issue (on the top left). Enter the feature summary and description. Do not forget to choose Type:Enhancement, instead of Type:Defect for one of the labels.
